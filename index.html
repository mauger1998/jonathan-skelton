Jonathan G Skelton | ARTIST & CALLIGRAPHER

Artist and calligrapher Jonathan G Skelton creates images in response to the landscape and to music. His twin practises of Tai Chi Chuan and Chinese calligraphy underpin the majority of his work. He also maintains skills in English calligraphy and heraldry and in illustration.

All works are on paper using Chinese inks, acrylics, including metallics, and emulsions. Paintings are mounted, have borders, are framed and unglazed. Artworks are for sale – please see Contact and Sales.
